Poosh

An all-new visual-challenge social networking app that offers users the ideal opportunity to showcase their talents and make friends, altogether while generating income either via winning cash rewards through challenges or by donations made through giveaways from loyal fans.

5W's Framework

Who

Content creators and individuals with access to social networking apps.

What

A new visual-challenge social networking app that offers users the ideal opportunity to showcase their talents and make friends, altogether while generating income via winning cash rewards through challenges or by donations made through giveaways from loyal fans.

When

Users can visit the social app at any time to either create content or enter a challenge pool to earn rewards.

Where

Users can access the app on any device with a connection to the internet.

Why

Users want an opportunity to earn a living through creating content while still networking in a friendly manner.

Problem Statement

According to a survey conducted, it revealed that most social media users create various viral video or photo challenges on their social media platforms, either on TikTok, Snapchat or Instagram, etc.

But a major setback is that the creators are rarely credited or influenced by their innovation. It showed that over 90% of content creators would love to have a social media platform which can generate a sort of income for their creativity while still maintaining a friendly, social interface.

Goals

1

Create a video-challenge social networking app, where challengers earn real cash rewards.

2

Creating a source of income for every user/customer of the app. (Side-job)

3

Cash Reward for users/content creators via challenges or Give away with E-wallet for depositing and saving cash rewards

Design Process

Double Diamond Approach

Using the double diamond design thinking approach, we were able to take the design challenge using the duality of divergent and convergent thinking.

Competitive Analysis

TikTok

Short-form Video Platform

TikTok is the Chinese creative social media platform primarily driven by short-form video content. It launches challenges of various types to tap into the creativity of its users and generate engaging (if not addicting content) accessible via an infinite feed.

Strengths

Market Share

Approximately 1.1 billion people are estimated to be active users of TikTok as of 2021. The video-sharing social media platform operates in over 154 countries across the globe, with approximately 60% of its users being Gen Z.

Free Service and Ease of Use

In the years before the introduction of TikTok, many video editing was done by professionals using very complex software. TikTok, however, allows users to quickly edit and create high quality short videos with ease. Additionally, it is free of charge, just download and start using the app.

User Safety and Privacy

Direct private messages cannot be sent to a content creator on TikTok if they are not a follower. However, the platform provides the content creator option in the settings whether to allow followers to leave messages or not. It demonstrates how much the platform values the privacy of its users.

Teamwork Culture

Friends, family, and acquaintances get together to make videos, which is a fun activity.

Weaknesses

No Equity in Income

Although there are ways to earn income on TikTok such as:

  • • Management services: Creating strategies to boost views and followers
  • • Donations: Coin donations from viewers via Live
  • • TikTok ads platform: Selling products via ads platform

However, the most prevailing is Product Influencing. The drawback is that users with smaller followings are underrepresented because they are less likely to land influencing gigs and are not able to monetize their content.

Addictive and Time Consuming

As much as the company wants to be profitable, nobody is really scrutinizing the users' mental health. According to a report by Wallaroomedia, the average TikTok user spends 52 minutes per day on the app. 90 minutes per day are wasted by kids between the ages of 4 and 15 on TikTok. However, they access the platform about 5 to 8 times per day.

Opportunities

Improve its reputation: Recently, TikTok has come under fire for its light-hearted content. Many people have criticized it, claiming that it harms society.

Threats

A controversy has arisen surrounding this app, which has sparked the suspicion of governments around the globe, who are debating whether to allow the app to operate in their countries.

Instagram

Visual Content Platform

Instagram is another social media platform driven by visual content for followers to interact with through likes, comments, and shares.

Strengths

Cross-Platform Integration

With Instagram, you can post photos and videos across multiple platforms easily.

Innovation Pioneer

As one of the first social media platforms dedicated exclusively to photos & videos, it is a great example of innovation in social media.

Massive User Base

The number of registered users on Instagram exceeds a billion.

Facebook Partnership

With Facebook as its parent company, Instagram can leverage its strengths. Its affiliation with Facebook has provided it with a strong talent pool, allowing it to continue growing.

Weaknesses

Privacy Concerns

Certain Instagram users have raised concerns about privacy and data leaks.

Market Share Growth

Limited market share growth due to many similar social media platforms e.g TikTok.

Opportunities
  • • Increase in smartphone users and internet usage
  • • Rapid technical advancement might aid in brand expansion
  • • More marketing and promotion can raise consumer awareness
Threats
  • • Innovation is essential to keep users interested
  • • Intense competition can erode user engagement of Instagram

User Research

Understanding User Needs

To better understand the pain points of users of social media apps, we collected over 70 user surveys on the current social media apps that were already functional. We gathered all the information and synthesized several root problems and potential solutions through product mapping and user journeys.

Personas

Using the statistics and data gleaned from user interviews and surveys, I created two personas that represented the target users. For example, Chloe is a representative of many young girls who is attempting to make it as influencers and use various social media apps to propel themselves to the spotlight. These personas were referred to throughout the entire app development process.

Proposed Solutions

Challenge Money Pool

Creating a money pool where different challengers can participate by placing a bet/bid on their respective visual-content entry with any amount of Poosh coins. Winners take a percentage of the lump sum based on their bet amount.

Give Away (Donations)

Creating a Poosh-coin feature where loyal fans can support their content-creators through donations. These coins are automatically converted into real cash rewards through the app's e-wallet feature. 50 Poosh coins = $2.

E-Wallet System

E-Wallet payment adds efficiency to the transaction process by eliminating the need for users to collect and process payments outside the app. Maximum security and transparency guaranteed.

Wireframes

During the ideation process of the design I created low-fidelity wireframes for testing purposes using Adobe XD. These wireframes helped establish the basic structure and user flow before moving to high-fidelity designs.

The wireframes focused on the core functionalities: challenge creation, user interaction, and the reward system interface.

Key Learnings

User Research Power

Never under-estimate the power of user and market research. This helps during feature prioritization.

Iteration is Key

Iterate as much as possible. Remember the product is for the end-user and not your personal project.

Embrace Feedback

Embrace feedback as it helps to improve the product and create better user experiences.
