Frequently Asked Questions

What's your design process?

I follow a structured 4-phase process: Discover (research & strategy), Define (problem identification), Design (ideation & creation), and Deliver (testing & implementation). Each project is tailored to your specific needs and goals.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple website redesign might take 4-6 weeks, while a comprehensive app design could take 8-12 weeks. I provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and am comfortable collaborating across different time zones. I use various communication tools to ensure smooth project management and regular updates.

What do you need to get started?

To begin, I need a clear understanding of your project goals, target audience, brand guidelines (if any), content, and any specific requirements or constraints. We'll discuss all of this in our initial consultation.
